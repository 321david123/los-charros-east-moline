Who We Are

A family kitchen
East Moline calls its own.

Los Charros is family-owned and family-run — and it shows in every detail. The cozy dining room is warmly decorated and lined with photographs that celebrate the owners' heritage, and the hands-on owner is singled out in review after review for his genuine kindness and the way he goes out of his way for every guest.

Every meal begins the right way: complimentary house chips with three made-from-scratch salsas — a bright red, a tangy tomatillo-green, and a creamy avocado. Our tacos come on real corn tortillas, our burritos are famously generous, and regulars come back again and again for the horchata and the tres leches cake.
